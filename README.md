## Key Insights
- 68% of global GDP controlled by just 10 countries ($70.86T)
- 88% of nations (159 countries) showing upward growth in 2023
- Asia leads global growth at 4.5% (weighted), driven by China (5.25%) and India (8.15%)
- US + China = 43.9% of world economy
- 147% gap between average ($17,711) and median ($7,180) GDP per capita
